Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

A chocolate chip cookie is a delectable baked good that satisfies the senses with its irresistible aroma, golden-brown color, and mouth-watering taste. This beloved treat typically comprises a delicate balance of butter, sugar, flour, eggs, and vanilla extract, which all blend together to form a heavenly dough. The crowning jewel of this dough, however, is the chocolate chips or chunks, which are generously scattered throughout the mixture to provide pockets of decadent, melty chocolate with every bite. When baked to perfection, these cookies boast a crispy exterior that gives way to a soft, chewy center, making them an ideal choice for any occasion, from a cozy night in to a festive celebration. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven or cooled to room temperature, chocolate chip cookies are a timeless favorite that never fail to delight the taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 3/4 cup white granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
